What is TGS?

Mission

The mission is simple: stop bad haircuts! TGS is a free service for the prospective barbershop patron dedicated to help men eradicate the bad haircut experience. The movement is a “shop local” mentality that custom matches men with the barbershops that best fit their preference and personality. TGS is committed to helping men discover the benefits of the barbershop experience, while delivering business to local barbershops! The mission is to elevate standards with a custom touch.

How it works

  1. From the home page click the “Get Started” button, which will lead you to the main survey page.
  2. Answer the 13 questions according to your preferences. Based on your answers to these questions, you will be matched to your top 3 correlating barbershops from our database.
  3. Lastly, simply click your desired barbershop, and we give you Their info!

The Team

We are three friends from high school that have a passion for men’s style and grooming. We love the barbershop experience, but struggled to find the shop that was the right fit for each of us. We have all gone to shops based on friend’s recommendations, customer reviews, and most dreaded: the chain shop! We created TGS out of a necessity. We needed to create a vehicle to deliver men to their custom barbershop. Here it is, enjoy!
